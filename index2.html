This is my first attempt at using CSS to put a box around text

I decided to make this box white (because that's is what I am most likely to use on my sites going forward) with a thin black boarder. I also made sure to center my text within the box with space for margins. I also played around with including both a header and body text within the same box!

I think this formatting will be very helpful for future sites, especially if they have a complicated background like the ombre one I used here.

Look! Here is another CSS box with prettier colors. The first time I added this green box I accidently turned the box above green as well. Then I realized that I had to make sure they has different classes... ie: "box" and "box2". Honestly, quite proud of figuring that one out on my own dispite it being a decently simple concept. It's about the little things in life I guess haha!

Just when you thought that boxes couldn't possibly get more interesting... I introduce box 3: the color changing box. I know, I know. Crazy. Groundbreaking. Life-altering. Religion-inspiring. Climate Crisis Reversing.

Being honest, this box took everything out of me. This will be the final box of this week for me.
